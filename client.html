About

In 1998, Ross started SureLock Storage with nothing more than a box, a lock and a dream. Ross would go down to the local YMCA and charge gym goers $2 to put their stuff in his box. 20 years later SureLock Storage storage is one of the leading companies in self-storage, with over 50 containers worldwide.

Mission

Our mission is to provide reliable, secure, and easily accessible storage solutions at an affordable rate

Vision

Our vision is to continuously invest in innovative storage technology and to expand our network of storage facilities.
